Live Music Visualizer (2018)

In November of 2018, I was involved in a 3-week long course focused on technology, music, and visual art, during which I created this piece of software, which is capable of translating input from an electronic musical instrument into a visual display of colors and shapes. The project took shape over several days and I documented my progress through video clips which I have pieced together for your viewing. In the final clip, I play Debussy's Clair de Lune visualized with one of my favorite configurations.
